About Edgecross

Edgecross is the birthplace of membrane contactor technology in China. Established in March 2013, we specialize in the research, development, production, and engineering of membrane contactors, complete skid systems, and integrated application solutions.

As a full-technology chain provider, we offer end-to-end solutions that enhance safety, improve energy efficiency, and prevent corrosion across the entire circulation system—from desalinated water to steam and condensate. Our innovations support industries in achieving their goals of energy savings, carbon reduction, and lower product unit consumption.

We are committed to delivering high-performance, sustainable technologies that transform industrial operations and contribute to a cleaner, smarter future.

Our Mission

To lead innovation in membrane contactor technology and provide integrated, high-performance solutions that drive safety, energy efficiency, and environmental sustainability. We support industries in optimizing their circulation systems while contributing to global energy conservation and carbon reduction efforts.

Our Vision

To be a global leader in membrane contactor technology and system integration, setting the standard for innovation, quality, and sustainability. We envision a future where industrial water and steam systems are safer, more efficient, and environmentally responsible—powered by Edgecross solutions.
