ETO Series Membrane Contactors

Our ETO series membrane contactors are specifically designed for clean water deoxygenation applications. With superior performance and reliability, these products are the ideal solution for industries requiring precise oxygen removal from process water.

Important Notes

  • This membrane contactor is only suitable for clean water deoxygenation applications.
  • Water should not contain oxidizing substances, such as hypochlorous acid, hydrogen peroxide, etc., otherwise the service life of the membrane will be shortened.

    • Boiler feed water systems

  • Wastewater Ammonia Removal (NH3 Removal)
    • Ammonia stripping in industrial wastewater
