About Edgecross

Edgecross is the birthplace of membrane contactor technology in China. Established in April 2013, we specialize in the research, development, manufacturing, and engineering of membrane contactors, degassing membranes, and integrated gas-liquid contacting systems. Our unique structure was granted a national patent in April 2014 and has been continuously improved, with second-generation products patented in December 2020.

Located in Suzhou, Jiangsu province, China, Edgecross is a professional company with membrane contactor/degassing membrane products and their applications as our core business. To date, we have sold over 6,000 contactors and 200+ gas-liquid contacting systems to customers worldwide, including South Korea, Japan, Vietnam, Malaysia, European Union, South Africa, and other regions. With an overseas office in Singapore, our sales and technical teams provide convenient international service.

Our products have been widely used for dissolved gas control of water in semiconductor, power generation, petrochemical, community heating, hot water corrosion resistance, and other industries for over 10 years. Even in critical applications with high reliability requirements, such as nuclear facilities and offshore oil & gas platforms, Edgecross products have demonstrated excellent performance.

As a full-technology chain provider, we offer end-to-end solutions that enhance safety, improve energy efficiency, and prevent corrosion across the entire circulation system—from desalinated water to steam and condensate. Our innovations support industries in achieving their goals of energy savings, carbon reduction, and lower product unit consumption.
